The Chainsmokers announce European tour and confirm new single

August 23, 2024
post

The Chainsmokers – made up of Drew Taggart and Alex Pall – have announced a headline European tour for 2020.

Kicking off in Munich on October 7, the duo will visit the Czech Republic, Italy, London, Dublin, Dusseldorf, Brussels and Amsterdam before finishing off in Paris on October 24.

The Chainsmokers European Tour 2020

Wed October 7 – Zenith, Munich
Thu October 8 – Tipsport Arena, Prague
Sat October 10 – Mediolanum Forum, Milan
Friday October 16 – O2 Academy Brixton, London
Sunday October 18 – 3Arena, Dublin
Wednesday October 21 – Mitsubishi Electric Halle, Dusseldorf
Thu October 22 – Forest National, Brussels
Fri October 23 – Ziggo Dome, Amsterdam
Sat October 24 – Zenith, Paris

Tickets for the tour will be available through Spotify Pre-Save on November 6, with a general sale starting on November 8. Click here for more information.

The Chainsmokers have also announced that they will be releasing a new single called ‘Push My Luck’ this Friday (November 8). The track forms part of their ongoing building album, World War Joy. The album already includes the previously released ‘Call You Mine’ featuring Bebe Rexha and ‘Who Do You Love’ featuring 5 Seconds of Summer.

Drew and Alex are currently in the middle of their huge WORLD WAR JOY tour across North America. Supported by 5 Seconds of Summer and Lennon Stella, the tour covers over 40 cities and will run through until December.
