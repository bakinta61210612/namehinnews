Armored Core 6 Battle Logs Locations (Combat Logs)

August 30, 2024
post

The Battle Logs are a side activity players can challenge while playing missions. In this guide, I will go over all the Battle Logs locations in Armored Core 6.

Through the LOGHUNT Mercenary Program, certain enemy mechs have been marked as targets. As you defeat those targets, and rank up you will a reward each time.

Do keep this in mind before tackling this. To fully gather all the battle logs you will need to have three full playthroughs or go up to New Game ++.

If you want to know more about the different endings, check out our guide on Decision Missions in Armored Core 6.

Armored Core 6: Battle Log Basics

Before going over all the locations let’s just take a look at the basics.

There are 4 types of combat logs: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. 

Mostly special enemies like ACs and other tougher MTs will have these combat logs, and you’ll acquire these logs by destroying these targets.

You can also find combat logs on regular enemies but you would have to fully explore certain areas during the mission to find them. The enemies you are looking for have an indicator to the top left of their health, and stagger bars.

Let’s say though that you complete a stage, and you couldn’t find the combat log. Don’t worry, because by simply going to the Replay Mission tab, you will be able to check.

Once you check the mission you want, on the right side you will see the Combat Zone section, the Objective section, and lastly the Rewards section. In the Rewards section, you will notice the Battle Log.

There are three things that it will:

  • Pending: There are enemies you have yet to defeat that possess a Battle Log.
  • Complete: You have completed all the Battle Log objectives.
  • Nothing: The particular stage does not have any combat log to offer.

Armored Core 6: Chapter 1 Combat Logs

Here are all the combat logs you will be able to find in Chapter 1 on your first playthrough.

Mission 4: Destroy Transport Helicopters

  • Mark: Tetrapod
  • After destroying the second Helicopter, you will need to go higher on the map and destroy the enemies close to the third and last helicopter.

Mission 5: Destroy the Tester AC

  • Mark: Enemy Armored Core
  • The mission’s objective, and you cannot miss it.

Mission 6: Attack the Dam Complex

  • Mark: Tetrapod
  • Between the second and third objectives, on the right of the frozen lake.
  • Mark: Enemy Armored Core
  • It will begin to attack you when you are close to finishing the mission. Destroy it before the final objective.

Mission 8: Climb the Wall

  • Mark: Three low-level enemy mechs
  • To the left side of the beginning area, before infiltrating the giant wall.

Mission 9: Retrieve Combat Logs

  • Mark: Enemy Armored Core
  • At the end of the mission destroy the enemy armored core that attacks you before time runs out.
  • Mark: Yue Yu/ Little Ziyi
  • When the mission begins so straight, you will see an open area with huge debris. Jump in, and you will find the enemy

Mission 11: Attack the Watchpoint

  • Mark: Sulla
  • Sulla is the mini-boss, and she is a part of the main mission so you can’t miss it.

Armored Core 6: Chapter 2 Combat Logs

Here are all the combat logs you will be able to find in Chapter 2 on your first playthrough.

Mission 12: Infiltrate Grid 086

  • Mark: “Invencible” Rummy
  • It is one of the objectives of the mission, you can’t miss it.
  • Mark: Two Tetrapods
  • After defeating Rummy, go through the door, and directly to the area above.
  • Mark: Two MT Units
  • Follow the rail line near the Tetrapods away from the objective, and look towards the left.
  • Mark: Enemy Armored Core
  • There is a secret room in the furnace room. There is a path inside the pipe to the right where the liquid metal is being poured.
  • Mark: Three MT Units
  • After you exit the furnace area, to the right of the building in front of you.

Mission 14: Ocean Crossing

  • Mark: Flying Drones
  • On the right side of the bridge just before the cargo platform. There should be a tunnel leading to half of them and a dead doser MT. The other half are hidden around the airspace near that area.

Armored Core 6: Chapter 3 Combat Logs

Here are all the combat logs you will be able to find in Chapter 3 on your first playthrough.

Mission 15: Steal the Survey Data

  • Mark: Heavy MT Unit
  • From the starting point of the mission, follow the road on the right. Keep going through it, and you will come across it

Mission 16: Attack the Refueling Base

  • Mark: LC Unit
  • Under the bridge you use in the middle section of the mission, to get to the valley check to the left of the bridge
  • Mark: LC Unit
  • In the valley, there are two thin bridges. Head to the further one, and it will fire lasers at you.

Mission 17: Eliminate V.VII

  • Mark: Tetrapod
  • Near the first checkpoint of the mission, on the ground level among the tall buildings.
  • Mark: V.VII Swinburne
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it.
  • Mark: Armored Core Rokumonsen
  • Accept Swinburne’s deal during the fight, and defeat the Armored Core that appears

Mission 18: Tunnel Sabotage

  • Mark: LC Unit
  • After the notification that the PCA is returning, go left into a room with a swarm of enemies, and defeat the one that fires lasers.

Mission 19: Survey the Uninhabited Floating City

  • Mark: Flying Unit
  • Facing the wrecked drone, turn left, and keep going forward until you find a circular building. Go to the top of this build and the mark will start to attack you.

Mission 23: Attack the Old Spaceport

  • Mark: Shielded Enemy
  • At the beginning of this mission go to the right, toward the higher area to the left of the hangar. Outside the hangar, you will find the mark.
  • Mark: Two LCs
  • In the fuel storage area, among the many fuel tanks.

Mission 24: Eliminate Honest Brute

  • Mark: Rolling Enemy
  • Head to the central pillar, by dropping down from the platform with the lasers. Close to the central pillar the mark in the one on the right.
  • Mark: Two Rolling Enemies
  • Around the pillar on the next platform.
  • Mark: Armored Tetrapod
  • On top of the large platform with the train turntable.
  • Mark: Rolling Enemy
  • Find the shield that is on the last platform. Go through it, and the mark is on the other side.
  • Mark: Honest Brute
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it.

Mission 25: Defend the Old Spaceport

  • Mark: Raven
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it.

Armored Core 6: Chapter 4 Combat Logs

Here are all the combat logs you will be able to find in Chapter 4 on your first playthrough.

Mission 29: Underground Exploration – Depth 2

  • Mark: G5 Iguazu
  • It is one of the objectives of the mission, you can’t miss it.
  • Mark: Multiple Enemies
  • To the right of the room, where blue lasers are shot toward you

Mission 30: Underground Exploration – Depth 3

  • Mark: Multiple Enemies
  • On the roof where the blue lasers are shot from.
  • Mark: Ayre
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it

Mission 31: Intercept the Redguns (Decision Mission) – If a player goes after this mission, Mission 32 will become unavailable

  • Mark: G1 Michigan
  • The target of the Decision Mission

Mission 32: Ambush the Vespers (Decision Mission) – If a player goes after this mission, Mission 31 will become unavailable

  • Mark: DUAL NATURE / V.VIII PATER and RECONFIG/V.V HAWKINS
  • The target of the Decision Mission

Mission 33: Unknown Territory Survey

  • Mark: V.IV Rusty
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it
  • Mark: TSUBASA/Middle Flatwell
  • Dropped during the boss fight

Mission 34: Reach the Coral Convergence

  • Mark: Two Enemy Armored Cores
  • At the beginning of the mission
  • Mark: Rolling Robot
  • Across the bridge

Armored Core 6: Chapter 5 Combat Logs

Here are all the combat logs you will be able to find in Chapter 5 on your first playthrough.

Mission 37: Intercept the Corporate Forces (Decision Mission) – If a player goes after this mission, Mission 38 will become unavailable

  • Mark: V.I Freud
  • The main objective of the mission, you can’t miss it

Mission 38: Eliminate “Cinder” Carla (Decision Mission) – If a player goes after this mission, Mission 37 will become unavailable

  • Mark: V.II Snail
  • It is one of the objectives of the mission, you can’t miss it.
  • Mark: FULL COURSE/Cinder Carla
  • It is one of the objectives of the mission, you can’t miss it.
  • Mark: CIRUS/Chatty Stick 
  • It is one of the objectives of the mission, you can’t miss it.

At this point in time Batlle Logs for the New Game Plus, and higher have not been discovered.

Final Thoughts

Side activities like this encourage players to explore the map and not tunnel vision towards only the objective.
