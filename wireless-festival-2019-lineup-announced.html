Wireless Festival 2019 lineup announced

August 24, 2024
post

Wireless Festival has unveiled its lineup for 2019 – with the hottest rappers and R&B singers taking to the stage.

Cardi B, Migos, Travis Scott and ASAP Rocky have been announced as headliners, with Cardi B and Migos co-headling on the Friday (5th July), Travis Scott performing on the Saturday (6th July), and ASAP Rocky on the Sunday (7th July).

As well as these urban superstars, the festival will also see performances from Future, Ella Mai, Tyga, Stefflon Don, Rae Sremmurd and Rich the Kid to name a few.

However, most tickets for the festival have already sold out, with only VIP tickets still available. If you still want a chance to attend the festival then click here to buy them. Buying these tickets will also give you access to an exclusive VIP village with cocktails, food trucks and luxury toilets!

Wireless Festival will take place on the first weekend of July (5th-7th) in Finsbury Park, North London.
