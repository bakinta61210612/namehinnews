The Cramps, pioneers of psychobilly and one of the most influential underground rock bands of the late 20th century, unleashed their raw energy and primal sound with the haunting track “Goo Goo Muck.”

Released in 1981, this rebellious anthem defied conventions and catapulted listeners into a twisted world of swampy guitars, rumbling basslines, and Lux Interior’s distinctive, devilish vocals.
