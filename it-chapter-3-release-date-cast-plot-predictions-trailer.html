IT Chapter 2 Recap:

IT Chapter Two, the 2019 American supernatural horror film directed by Andy Muschietti, takes viewers on a terrifying journey inspired by Stephen King’s 1986 novel. The sequel picks up 27 years after the first film, with the now-adult Losers Club returning to their hometown of Derry to confront their deepest fears and defeat Pennywise once and for all.

The group must overcome the loss of one of their own as they embark on a dangerous mission to collect tokens from their past and perform a ritual to destroy the malevolent entity. But when the ritual fails, they must band together and use their strength to make Pennywise seem small and vulnerable. In a heart-pounding finale, they succeed in defeating the monster by crushing its heart and saving its hometown from its grasp.

IT Chapter 2 Review:

IT Chapter Two garnered mixed reactions from critics, but hold on to your seats because this sequel still delivers on its scares while staying faithful to the source material. With an impressive 62% rating on Rotten Tomatoes, the movie has been described as “proving bigger doesn’t always mean scarier for horror sequels, but a fine cast and faithful approach to the source material keep this follow-up afloat”.

IGN raved about the solid performances that bring this saga to an effective conclusion, although they highlighted some pacing issues and sub-standard CGI. Overall, if you’re looking for an engaging horror flick with memorable set-pieces, It Chapter Two is definitely worth the watch.
