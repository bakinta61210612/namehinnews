Just days after Bobbi Kristina Brown‘s death at age 22, the late Whitney Houston‘s friend Tyler Perry is speaking out about the young woman’s tragic passing.

“I’m a writer with no words…. Bobbi Kris,” the 45-year-old actor wrote on Twitter on Tuesday, July 28.
