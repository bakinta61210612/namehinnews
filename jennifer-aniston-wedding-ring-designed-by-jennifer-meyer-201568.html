Jennifer Aniston's Wedding Ring Was Designed by Bestie Jennifer Meyer

September 09, 2024
post

With this ring! Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux put rings on it on Wednesday, Aug. 5, in a top-secret wedding, and Us Weekly can confirm that the baubles in question were designed by none other than the actress’ good friend Jennifer Meyer.

In case you didn’t know, the jewel maker, 38, who married Tobey Maguire in 2007, has bedazzled the ears, necks, and wrists of Hollywood’s finest, including Beyonce (with a custom “Blue” ring), Blake Lively, Emma Stone, and Jessica Biel. She frosted Kerry Washington for the 2014 Oscars, and even crafted Courtney Cox‘s engagement ring.

And now, she can add “Jennifer Aniston’s wedding ring designer” to her resume, too. But this isn’t the first time she’s glammed up the Friends alum! Aniston has rocked Meyer’s dainty pendants—most often the “Wishbone Necklace”—on multiple occasions, including on the big screen in The Break-Up and He’s Just Not That Into You.

Even better, the designer scored an invite to Aniston’s hush-hush nuptials. The morning after the couple of four years tied the knot at their home in L.A., Meyer took to Instagram to share a pic of what appeared to be her flirty ensemble for the occasion, which she captioned: “Last night in my favorite dress… #xojm”
