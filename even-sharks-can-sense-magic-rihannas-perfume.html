Even Sharks Can Sense the Magic of Rihannas Perfume

August 21, 2024
post

Rihanna seems to have her hands in everything these days.  While the 26 year old pop star has spent years topping charts across the world, she refuses to allow her career to be one dimensional.  Over the last several years, Rihanna has embarked on other branding opportunities and in 2011 she released her first fragrance, “Reb’l Fleur.”  Since then, Rihanna has released three other fragrances including her most recent, “Rogue Man.”  The response to Ri Ri’s scents has been largely positive with products selling out almost immediately after their release. Although Rihanna’s fragrances are obviously meant to be worn, PerfumesbyRihanna.com encourages customers to, “push boundaries,” something Rihanna is well known for doing herself. Several customers have taken it upon themselves to do just that.  By posing bottles of Rihanna’s perfume next to various items, Instagram users around the world have shown that Rihanna’s fragrances defy all boundaries, and can go with just about anything.  Here are pictures of Rihanna’s perfume(s) alongside some unlikely co-stars.

4,  Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur” goes to  Starbucks.

Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur” looks pretty fancy next to this frozen drink from Starbucks.

3. Marilyn Monroe meets Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur.”

Posed next to this image of the iconic Marilyn Monroe, Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur” looks elegant and luxurious.

2.  Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur” and the skull.

While it may be too late for the skull to appreciate the scent of Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur,” the two still look interesting posed side by side in this picture.

1. A shark takes a selfie with Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur.”

Even sharks are attracted to Rihanna’s “Reb’l Fleur.”  Sharks, who are known or their keen sense of smell, can apparently sniff out the scent through thick aquarium glass.
