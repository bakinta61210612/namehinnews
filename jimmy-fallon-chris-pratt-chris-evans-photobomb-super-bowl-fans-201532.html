One smokin’ surprise! A group of lucky Super Bowl fans got an unexpected visit this past weekend from three Hollywood hunks. 

Jimmy Fallon, Chris Pratt, and Chris Evans snuck up on football revelers ahead of the Seattle Seahawks game against the winning New England Patriots. As couples, individuals, and groups of friends posed in front of a step-and-repeat emblazoned with the NBC Sports logo and that of Super Bowl XLIX, the trio did some serious photobombing.

In a special Super Bowl edition of “Tonight Show Celebrity Photobomb,” Fallon, 40, enlisted Seahawks loyalist Pratt, 35, and Boston fan Evans, 33. The three stars hit Phoenix, Ariz. for a little fun on the NBC Super Bowl Red Carpet.
