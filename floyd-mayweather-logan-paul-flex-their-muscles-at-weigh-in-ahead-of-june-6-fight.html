Floyd Mayweather &amp; Logan Paul Flex Their Muscles at Weigh-In Ahead of June 6 Fight

September 01, 2024
post

Floyd Mayweather and Logan Paul‘s highly anticipated fight is just hours away and they had their weigh-in ahead of the big day.

The 44-year-old pro boxer and the 26-year-old social media star appeared at the weigh-in event on Saturday afternoon (June 5) at Seminole Hard Rock Casino in Miami Gardens, Fla.

Floyd and Logan both put their ripped bodies on display while going shirtless during the weigh-in.

The pay-per-view event will feature four fights and fans can buy tickets for $49.99 through Showtime and Fanmio.

Floyd is coming out of retirement and risking his undefeated 50-0 record to fight against Logan. He previously retired after his 2017 match with Conor McGregor, which earned him $275 million thanks to his roles as both fighter and promoter.
