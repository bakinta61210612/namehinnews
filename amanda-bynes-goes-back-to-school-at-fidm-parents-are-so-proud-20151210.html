Amanda Bynes Goes Back to School at FIDM, Parents Are &quot;So Proud&quot;

September 08, 2024
post

Amanda Bynes is once again pursuing her dream of a career in fashion. After some time off in recent months, the former child star is back in school at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM), her parents' lawyer Tamar Arminak confirms to Us Weekly. 

Bynes, 29, first enrolled at the school in early 2014 but stopped attending classes that fall, shortly before she was placed under a psychiatric hold for several weeks. She has since returned to the L.A. campus. 

"We are all so proud of her, and can't wait to see her amazing designs on the runway someday," Arminak tells Us of the Easy A actress.

Bynes' erratic behavior has been well-documented over the past few years, but she's kept a much lower profile since leaving treatment last year. Shortly after her release, she tweeted that she was still seeing doctors weekly and taking medication.
