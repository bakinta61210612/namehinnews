David Mark Bulley announces headline New York show

August 25, 2024
post

British singer-songwriter David Mark Bulley has announced a headline show in New York, which forms part of his 2018 The Confessions Tour.

David will perform at The Loft on Saturday April 7, alongside special guest Jason Lapin. Tickets for the New York show will cost $15 and go on sale on Friday November 10 at 9am ET. They will be available to purchase from his official webstore.

Speaking about the show, David said: “New York has a very special place in my heart. Being able to bring my tour here is a dream come true. It will be a show I will never forget.”

Check out the official tour poster below.

The New York show is the latest date to be added to David’s 2018 The Confessions Tour. He will be performing at The Buyers Club, Liverpool on February 17 and Surya, London on February 18 alongside a sold out show in Little Temple Bar, Paris on March 3. More dates are set to be added over the coming weeks.

2017 has been a truly exciting year for David. Back in March, he released the Purge inspired visual for his single, ‘Where Do We Go From Here’, which has since amassed over 152,000 views. The track has also received radio airplay across the world, including Australia.

The visual was followed up by the release of David’s debut album Prologue. The 12-track offering gave a real insight into the singer-songwriter’s life, weaving together his personal experiences to create a strong body of work.

In terms of being on the road, David has been busy performing in Poland since February. After joining Sylwia Przybysz on two legs of her Tylko Raz tour, he is currently supporting Jeremi and Artur Sikorski on their Odetchnij tour. David has also appeared at numerous UK shows including Elite Talent, Burton Fest and CLD Music.

David is busy working on his new album, which is set for release in Spring 2018. Fans who attend The Confessions Tour can expect to hear some of his previously released tracks, in addition to teasers from the new album.

With new music on the way and a huge headline tour on the horizon, 2018 looks set to be a stellar year for David Mark Bulley.
