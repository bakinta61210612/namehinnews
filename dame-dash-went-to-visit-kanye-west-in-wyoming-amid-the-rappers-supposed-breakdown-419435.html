Dame Dash Went To Visit Kanye West In Wyoming Amid The Rappers Supposed Breakdown

September 03, 2024
post

As Kanye West appears to be going through a breakdown – at least as far as social media and the mainstream media is concerned – many of his friends in the entertainment business have visited him, showing they are, in fact, in his corner even during the bad times.

Earlier this week, Kanye West released a series of messages about his mother-in-law, Kris Jenner, as well as his wife, Kim Kardashian, in which he slammed both of them for various transgressions. For instance, Kanye accused Kris of avoiding his phone calls and also said her and “calmye” weren’t allowed to be around his kids.

Regarding Kim, Kanye said it was clear to everyone that the notorious movie, Get Out, was about him. Furthermore, the rapper delivered a speech in which he claimed he and Kim considered aborting their first daughter together, North.

Since then, many of the rapper’s friends and cohorts in the music business have reached out to him, including Dame Dash. Hot New Hip Hop says the music mogul went to Wyoming earlier this week to hang out with the rapper.

Fans of the pair know they have a history together that goes many years into the past. Initially, the reports surrounding Kanye were mixed, with many arguing his media-headline grabbing behavior was a marketing campaign for a new record.
