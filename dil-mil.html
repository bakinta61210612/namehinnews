Dil Mil is a free dating application that lets you meet single South Asian men and women. The app boasts a history of over 20 million matches already created. With over two million singles with a profile, the application's small community still provides many opportunities to find a partner.

Dil Mil is one of many dating apps that uses the popular swipe mechanic to match with other people. If you're looking for a partner, Tinder, Bumble, and OkCupid are other great alternatives. Dil Mil isn't focused on hookups or arranged marriages and instead, promotes a safe space for men and women to find potential life partners.

Meet South Asians singles

Dil Mil is an app designed for South Asian singles, so it boasts a tight-knit community of this one ethnicity. With over two million users online, the application is filled with users ready to match. 

Interface

The Dil Mil app uses an interface that displays each user's picture and name, similar to most alternatives. However, the interface is difficult to navigate at times. 

Your profile

As you use the app and create a profile, you'll need to complete a series of tasks to stand out. Editing your bio requires you to answer a series of prompts, including your height, age, and gender. You'll also need to upload a picture of yourself to complete your profile.

VIP Elite

VIP Elite is the premium subscription for Dil Mil. The service requires a monthly subscription cost and will automatically renew unless it’s canceled. 

This hefty fee comes with many in-app features. These features include instant matches, boosts, unlimited likes, the option to go back on swipes, and a way to change your location. These features provide an easier way to find a match and start talking with other users quickly.

Our take

Dil Mil has a smaller community than many other dating apps on the market. However, the small community isn't a negative aspect, as it's focused solely on South Asians, providing a meeting place and a way to find others quickly. However, the database of users isn't tiny; with over two million users online, a potential match is just a swipe away.

Should you download it?

Yes. If you're specifically looking for South Asian singles, this app is a great option to install over any others.
