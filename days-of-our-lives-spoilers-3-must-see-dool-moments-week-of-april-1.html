Days of Our Lives spoilers reveal there are three must-see moments for the week of April 1, 2024. Expect Tate Black (Jamie Martin Mann) to finally come home.

Xander Cook (Paul Telfer) discovers a clue in his criminal case, which he brings to Rafe Hernandez (Galen Gering). Also, Tripp Johnson (Lucas Adams) realizes Wendy Shin (Victoria Grace) has post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

Tate Black’s Homecoming

DOOL spoilers for the week of April 1 reveal that one must-see moment is Tate’s homecoming. As fans began to see in Friday’s episode, Holly Jonas (Ashley Puzemis) finally told the truth about New Year’s Eve. Once Holly admits that the drugs were hers, Tate will be cleared and can finally go home.

DOOL Spoilers: Holly Jonas’ New Secret

In the weekly preview clip, Brady Black (Eric Martsolf) and Theresa Donovan (Emily O’Brien) bring Tate home. It looks like Tate might be staying at John Black (Drake Hogestyn) and Marlena Evans’ (Deidre Hall) penthouse.

In any case, Brady and Theresa sternly warn Tate that he’s not allowed to see or talk to Holly.

However, Tate sneaks into the DiMera mansion, where Holly passionately kisses him. So, it sounds like that might be connected to why Nicole Walker-DiMera (Arianne Zucker) hands down such a harsh punishment to Holly.

Xander Cook’s Criminal Investigation

Days of Our Lives spoilers for the week of April 1 reveal that Xander will look into his criminal case. Xander starts to really examine the evidence against him.

As Xander and Sarah Horton (Linsey Godfrey) peer at a computer screen, they find a disturbing clue. “Xarah” brings this clue to Rafe.
