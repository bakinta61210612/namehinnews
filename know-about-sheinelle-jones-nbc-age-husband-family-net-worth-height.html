Sheinelle Jones Bio; Parents, Education, Height

The Philadelphia native was born as Sheinelle Marie Jones on 19 April 1978 to parents C. Darnell Jones II and Sheila Kinnard. Her father is a United States District Judge of the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania.
