Adina Abramowitz Managing Partner At CDFI Friendly America Died

August 31, 2024
post

We regret to announce the passing of Adina Abramowitz, a prominent figure in community development finance and the Managing Partner at CDFI Friendly America. The news has deeply saddened the community and the Germantown Jewish Center, where Abramowitz was an active member.

Abramowitz’s dedication to community empowerment was evident in her work at CDFI Friendly America. The organization is a strategic initiative that aims to extend the reach of CDFI capital and knowledge to areas that are not yet well served. Her leadership and vision have shaped the trajectory of the organization and made a significant impact on various communities.

Over the years, Abramowitz found fulfillment working with individual CDFIs. Her role at CDFI Friendly America provided her a fresh perspective on community development, a cause she passionately advocated for.

Our condolences go out to Abramowitz’s family, friends, and colleagues during this challenging time. The Germantown Jewish Center joins the wider community in mourning her loss and honoring her enduring legacy.
